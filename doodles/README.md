# Doodles

This is where little exploratory notes and mini reports go when they are too useful
to throw away, but too scrappy to pretend they are Serious Documentation.

Think of it as the notebook margin of the repo:
- quick experiments
- small comparisons
- "wait, what actually happens if..."
- evidence gathered before making a choice

Mostly: messing around with data until the repo reveals its secrets.

Also, yes, `doodles/` is a fun name, and it earns bonus points for being easy to
reach with `d<tab>`.
